About Klemont Avalanche Safety(KAS)

At Klemont Avalanche Safety, we are dedicated to educating and equipping outdoor enthusiasts with the knowledge and tools needed to stay safe in avalanche-prone terrain. Our mission is to provide expert guidance, up-to-date resources, and hands-on training to help skiers, snowboarders, and backcountry adventurers make informed decisions. With a team of experienced professionals and a passion for mountain safety, we strive to empower individuals with the skills to assess risks, respond effectively, and enjoy the outdoors responsibly. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, Klemont Avalanche Safety is here to help you stay prepared and confident in the backcountry.
